## Development Roadmap

| Phase | Timeline | Goal |
|-------|----------|------|
| **1. Demo** | 12-18 mo | UHF CubeSat protocol validation |
| **2. Standards** | 24-36 mo | CCSDS Green Book, industry working group |
| **3. Regulatory** | 36-48 mo | ITU WRC-27 X-band allocation |
| **4. Production** | 48+ mo | Commercial service launch |

**Phase 1 proves:** Protocol correctness, multi-hop routing, atomic settlement

**Phase 2 establishes:** Industry standard, interoperability

**Phase 3 secures:** Spectrum for high-bandwidth ISL

**Phase 4 delivers:** Revenue-generating service
