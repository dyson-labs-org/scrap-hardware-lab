# SCRAP/SISL Academic Partnerships

Last updated: 2025-12-29

This document identifies potential academic partners for SCRAP (Secure Capabilities and Routed Authorization Protocol) and SISL (Secure Inter-Satellite Link) research and development.

---

## Why Academic Partnerships?

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| NSF Access | Many NSF programs require academic PI |
| Research Credibility | Peer-reviewed publications strengthen proposals |
| Student Pipeline | Graduate students for implementation work |
| Facilities | Labs, testbeds, ground stations |
| License Exceptions | EAR TSR exception for fundamental research |
